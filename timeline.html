8-12 Week Construction Timeline

Phased approach to minimize worship disruptions

Week 1-2: Protecting God's House

Weatherproofing the sanctuary

  • Site preparation and protection
  • Roof inspection and repairs
  • Waterproofing application for year-round worship
  • First coat of roof sealing

Week 2-3: Power for Ministry

Installing reliable electrical infrastructure

  • Electrical conduit installation
  • Distribution board setup for worship services
  • Cable pulling and routing
  • First safety inspection

Week 3-4: Emergency Worship System

Blackout support installation

  • Plumbing pipe installation
  • Solar geyser mounting (2 x 200L units)
  • Connection of hot water lines for fellowship meal support
  • Pressure testing and certification

Week 4-6: Extending the Main Hall

Creating space for larger congregations

  • Steel framework for hall expansion
  • Ceiling board mounting for worship acoustics
  • Cornice installation for finished appearance
  • Ceiling finishing and sanding

Week 6-7: Lighting for Worship

Completing electrical and lighting systems

  • Energy-efficient LED light fitting installation
  • Plug and switch installation for ministry equipment
  • Testing and certification
  • Final electrical inspection

Week 7-9: Durable Sanctuary Flooring

Long-lasting surfaces for active ministry

  • Floor preparation and leveling
  • Tile laying and alignment throughout sanctuary
  • Grouting and sealing for easy maintenance
  • Cleaning and protection

Week 8-10: Sacred Space Atmosphere

Creating peaceful worship environment

  • Wall preparation and priming
  • Interior wall painting in peaceful colors (2 coats)
  • Ceiling painting for brightness (2 coats)
  • Exterior painting for welcoming appearance (2 coats)

Week 10-11: Prayer Room Privacy

Securing sacred spaces for contemplation

  • Door frame installation for prayer rooms
  • Door hanging and adjustment for privacy
  • Hardware installation (locks for security)
  • Final adjustments

Week 11-12: Dedication Preparation

Completing the sanctuary for blessing

  • Touch-up painting throughout
  • Plumbing fixture installation (taps, fittings)
  • Final deep cleaning and consecration preparation
  • Inspection and handover for worship services
